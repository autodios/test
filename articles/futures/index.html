Laboratory of Possibilities

30. August 2014

Collabortive drawing + storytelling project about California’s future, created for the Oakland Museum of California’s Natural Science Gallery. Visitors to the museum can add a drawing or idea at one of several touch-screen stations, and immediately see their creations added to the projected wall of ideas.

Software Development and Interface Design: Olivia Jack
Graphic Identity: Joe Bromley, Auburn Lahoski
Initial Concept: Don Pohlman
Prototyping: Melissa Standen
